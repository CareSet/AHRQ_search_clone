Measure Title:
Performing cystoscopy at the time of hysterectomy for pelvic organ prolapse to detect lower urinary tract injury: percentage of patients who undergo cystoscopy to evaluate for lower urinary tract injury at the time of hysterectomy for pelvic organ prolapse.
HMIS Number:
004989
Measure Denominator:
The number of patients undergoing hysterectomy for pelvic organ prolapse

Hysterectomy (identified by Current Procedural Terminology [CPT] codes) performed for the indication of pelvic organ prolapse (identified by supporting International Classification of Diseases, Ninth Revision [ICD-9]/International Classification of Diseases, Tenth Revision [ICD-10] codes).
