Measure Title:
Functional Status Improvement for Patients who Received a Total Knee Replacement: Average change in functional status assessment score for patients 19 years and older who received an elective primary total knee arthroplasty (TKA) and completed a functional status assessment within 90 days prior to their surgery and in the 270-365 days after their surgery.
HMIS Number:
004267
Measure Denominator:
Patients must meet the following criteria to be counted in the measure population: --A patient-reported functional status assessment administered in the 90 days prior to or including the day of surgery and score recorded in the EHR --A patient-reported functional status assessment administered during the 270-365 days after surgery and score recorded in the EHR The following functional status assessment scores and their results should be recorded in the EHR (patient is expected to complete all subscales from at least one instrument): -Measure Population 1: Patients with a KOOS QoL (Quality of Life) Score in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a KOOS QoL (Quality of Life) Score in the 270-365 days after surgery -Measure Population 2: Patients with a KOOS Sport/Rec (Sport Recreation) Score in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a KOOS Sport/Rec (Sport Recreation) Score in the 270-365 days after surgery -Measure Population 3: Patients with a KOOS ADL (Activities of daily living) Score in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a KOOS ADL (Activities of daily living) Score in the 270-365 days after surgery -Measure Population 4: Patients with a KOOS Symptoms Score in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a KOOS Symptoms Score in the 270-365 days after surgery -Measure Population 5: Patients with a KOOS Pain Score in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a KOOS Pain Score in the 270-365 days after surgery -Measure Population 6: Patients with a PROMIS10 Global Mental Health (GMH) T Score in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a PROMIS10 Global Mental Health (GMH) T Score in the 270-365 days after surgery -Measure Population 7: Patients with a PROMIS10 Global Physical Health (GPH) T Score in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a PROMIS10 Global Physical Health (GPH) T Score in the 270-365 days after surgery -Measure Population 8: Patients with a VR12 Physical Summary Score (PCS) in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a VR12 Physical Summary Score (PCS) in the 270-365 days after surgery -Measure Population 9: Patients with a VR12 Mental Summary Score (MCS) in the 90 days prior to or including the day of the surgery and a VR12 Mental Summary Score (MCS) in the 270-365 days after surgery

Exclusions: The following criteria excludes patients from this measure: --An acute fracture of the knee or lower limb at the time of the TKA, or --A severe dementia diagnosis that overlaps the measurement period or the TKA surgery
Measure Numerator:
Average change in functional status assessment score after surgery for all patients in the measure population The change score is calculated for each instrument and subscale listed below: Measure Observations 1: KOOS QoL (Quality of Life) Score Measure Observations 2: KOOS Sport/Rec (Sport Recreation) Score Measure Observations 3: KOOS ADL (Activities of daily living) Score Measure Observations 4: KOOS Symptoms Score Measure Observations 5: KOOS Pain Score Measure Observations 6: PROMIS10 Global Mental Health (GMH) T Score Measure Observations 7: PROMIS10 Global Physical Health (GPH) T Score Measure Observations 8: VR12 Physical Summary Score (PCS) Measure Observations 9: VR12 Mental Summary Score (MCS)
