Measure Title:
Human Papillomavirus Vaccine (HPV): The percentage of adolescents age 13 who have had three doses of the human papillomavirus (HPV) vaccine by their 13th birthday
HMIS Number:
004457
Measure Denominator:
Patients eligible for inclusion in the denominator include: [Question 1] Is this a patient whose care is managed within the physician group? Patients who had at least two Primary Care office visits, regardless of diagnosis code, on different dates of service, to a PCP in the past 24 months (Eligibility Window). [Question 2] Is this a patient currently managed in our system? Patients who had at least one Primary Care office visit regardless of diagnosis code, with a PCP in the most recent 12 months (Measurement Period). [Question 3] Is this a patient that is eligible for an immunization? Patients who have documented anaphylactic reactions to the vaccine or its components prior to their 13th birthday should be excluded from this measure. The organizations may look for this exclusion as far back as possible in the patient s history, through administrative data and/or medical record review. The exclusion can be identified through an ICD-9 or ICD-10 diagnosis-based problem list. The problem must be ACTIVE. There is no limit on the look back date, but the date of documentation or onset date must occur prior to the end of the measurement period.

Exclusions: As indicated in Question 3 above: Patients who have documented anaphylactic reactions to the vaccine or its components prior to their 13th birthday should be excluded from this measure. The organizations may look for this exclusion as far back as possible in the patient s history, through administrative data and/or medical record review. The exclusion can be identified through an ICD-9 or ICD-10 diagnosis-based problem list. The problem must be ACTIVE. There is no limit on the look back date, but the date of documentation or onset date must occur prior to the end of the measurement period.
Measure Numerator:
Adolescents in the denominator who had at least three HPV vaccinations, with different dates of service, on or between the patient s 9th and 13th birthdays. HPV vaccinations administered prior to a patient s 9th birthday cannot be counted. HPV Vaccinations: Evidence of vaccinations can be demonstrated through the following: 1. Administrative Data which can include: a. Table HPV-3, using administration date of the vaccination. 2. Medical Record Review (refer to Medical Record Review for Numerator Inclusion/Denominator)
